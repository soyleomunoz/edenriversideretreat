Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is it from Santiago?

Eden Riverside Retreat is located in Isla de Maipo, just 40 minutes from Santiago city center by car. It's the perfect day trip or overnight escape from the capital.

Can I pay in USD?

Yes! We use Stripe for all online payments, which accepts international cards and allows you to pay in USD. No Chilean bank account or local currency is required.

Do I need to bring my own tent?

You are welcome to bring your own tent. We also have equipment rental options available. Contact us in advance to arrange tent hire and ensure availability.

Can we organize events and group activities?

Absolutely. We host corporate retreats, birthday parties, school trips, and private celebrations. We have large open spaces, a football pitch, BBQ areas and can adapt the venue to your event's needs.

Is there public transport to the camping?

The easiest way is by car or private transfer. We can help connect you with our local partner transport services from Santiago. Ask us via WhatsApp for current transfer options.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a partial charge. Contact us for special circumstances.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, well-behaved pets are welcome at Eden Riverside Retreat. We ask that they remain on a leash in shared areas and that owners clean up after them. Let us know when booking.

Are you open all year round?

Yes! We are open 365 days a year, 24 hours a day. Chile's mild Maipo Valley climate makes outdoor activities enjoyable throughout most of the year.

How to Get There

We're easy to reach from Santiago — just 40 minutes away.

By Car from Santiago

Take Ruta 5 Sur towards Buin, then exit towards Isla de Maipo. Follow signs to La Villita. Address: Calle Crucero 488, La Villita. Only 40 minutes from downtown Santiago.

Private Transfer

We partner with local transfer companies to offer direct pick-up from Santiago hotels and the airport. Ask us via WhatsApp to arrange your transfer.

Tour Agencies & Operators

We welcome travel agencies and tour operators to include us in their itineraries. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities and group rates.
